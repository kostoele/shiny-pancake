Spreewald

The Spreewald is an extensive lowland area and a historic cultural landscape in the southeast of the state of Brandenburg . The main feature is the natural river branch of the Spree , which has been significantly expanded by established channels. As an alluvial and moor landscape , it is of national importance for nature conservation and is protected as a biosphere reserve (see Spreewald Biosphere Reserve ). The Spreewald as a cultural landscape was crucial by the Sorbsembossed. The area is one of the most famous and popular destinations in the state of Brandenburg.
