Spreewald - Zurück zum Ich

The Spreewald Winter has its own charm. Experience an unforgettable winter holiday in the Spreewald! You can experience the white fairy-tale landscape in the Spreewald with mulled wine train rides or skating! Long-lasting frosty temperatures make the region a paradise for ice hikers and skaters. Ski slopes? Chairlifts? Instead: extended winter walks on clear air, rides on the frozen sledges. And then? Enjoy a bit of wellness in the Spreewald, the soothing warmth of a bath or massages with Spreewald herbs. Find peace - back to the me! The winter holiday in the Spreewald is pure deceleration, because a relaxed silence is spreading throughout the region. Attractive offers make the holiday booking easy.

Winterkahnfahrten im Spreewald

From November to March the Spreewald is winter season. In the cold season, the popular Glühweinkahnfahrten, as the winter barge drives are called in the Spreewald, start only about twice a day at selected ports. With cozy Blankets and hot drinks on board it goes by romantic winter boat trip through the snowy nature.              Now in winter, the silence of nature is almost audible, because in the Spreewald it is very quiet after the peak season. Enjoy the uniqueness of a boat trip in the off-season, because you are almost alone on the flow of the region.              Tip: Not all harbors offer daily winter barge rides in the low season.
