Spreewald Welcomes You

The Spreewald Winter has its own charm. Experience an unforgettable winter holiday in the Spreewald! You can experience the white fairy-tale landscape in the Spreewald with mulled wine train rides or skating! Long-lasting frosty temperatures make the region a paradise for ice hikers and skaters. We exchange the common Ski slopes and Chairlifts experience with the unique extended winter walks on clear air, rides on the frozen sledges.
Enjoy every bit of wellness in the Spreewald and the soothing warmth of a bath or massages with Spreewald herbs. The winter holiday in the Spreewald is pure deceleration, because a relaxed silence is spreading throughout the region. Our special offers make the holiday booking easy.

Winter Boat rides in Spreewald

From November to March in the Spreewald is winter season. In the winter season, the popular "Glühweinkahnfahrten", as the winter boats are called in the Spreewald, rides only about twice a day at selected ports. We have cozy Blankets and hot drinks on board for cushion on this romantic winter boat trip through the snowy nature. In winter, the silence of nature is almost audible, as tourism in the Spreewald hits the lowest. You should take advantage and enjoy the uniqueness of a boat trip in the off-season, because you are almost alone on the rivers of the region. Tip: Not all ports offer daily winter boat rides in the low season.
